FAQ

Here you can get answers for most of your questions. If something is unclear, send me an email and I will be happy to reply.

1. Is the price for a single miniature or a unit?

- The price in all categories is per one miniature.

2. Do you take commissions?

- Yes! If you need painting, converting, sculpting, assembling, cleaning, literally anything regarding the hobby, don't hesitate - simply contact me via ........

3. Do you order the models?

- Yes I do! I order them with 15-20% off retail price.

4. What information do you need to prepare a precise quote?

- Your name (first and last), email, list of models you are interested in (including their weapon loadout, magnetization etc) and their color scheme (including base theme).

5. Do you ship internationally?

- Yes. I have clients from US, Canada, Europe and Australia.

6. Do you provide safe packaging? Will my models be safe?

- Yes! I take very good care of your models and pack them very carefully.

7. Is there a minimum commission cost?

- No, there is not. You can order whatever you want, from single miniature to whole armies!

8. Can I get a tutorial how you painted my models?

- It all depends. If the process is too complicated I would have to charge for it extra.
